Food Products to Forage Online

  Our new social distancing world has made shopping in groceries from a luxury to a near-essential. The virus not only infected us, but businesses as well, many small retailers had to remain closed. Some businesses have started offering home delivery of fresh quality food products safely. Here are some businesses to buy some local food products safely.

Ichibake

  Ichibake features handcrafted French-Japanese fusion bakery and pastry products. They perform commercial and private event caterings, wholesale to local and overseas and offer cooking classes to individuals and groups. Ichibake specializes in their featured collection.

  Canelés are small fancy French pastries, originally from Bordeaux region of France. Flavoured with rum and vanilla with a soft and tender custard center and a dark, thick caramelised crust. They offer an assortment of types of flavours such as Orange Chocolate ($5), Tiramisu ($5) and Uji Matcha Red bean ($5). Ichibake also offers sharing boxes, an assortment of flavours, such as the Assorted Canele sharing box ($35) that comes with 8 flavours, lemon ginger, English earl grey, Kyoto Hojicha, black sesame, tiramisu, orange chocolate, white chocolate pistachio, Japanese kinako, one of each.

  Melon Pans are Japanese sweet buns originating from Japan. Made from enriched flavoured dough covered in a thin layer of sugar and a flavoured melon custard filling. They offer an assortment of types such as Hojicha ($4.75), cream cheese ($4.75), and their original melon pan ($4.75). Ichibake also offers sharing box, that come with 8 assorted melon Pans ($34).

  Ichibake requires a $20 minimum order, and charge a $5 flat delivery across GTA. Free delivery on orders $50 and above. Store pickup is available on two stores at:

  • 135 Ossington, Toronto, M6J 2Z6
  • 37 Baldwin St, Toronto, M5T 1L1


Graze and Gather

  Some of the favourite dishes are the Tajine, Chicken and Mixed Grains ($50) which is from the company Constantine. The dish serves 2-3 people and is a perfect choice for a dinner date at home.

  The produce cheese from Ontario Cheese Union is also a must buy. The cheese is handpicked and is easily one of the best family owned dairy farm producer. The 18 month aged Parmesan cheese is filled with nutty flavours and grassy undertones giving away a creamy mouth to savour. This is the BEST cheese to try out, especially for pizza. It is good quality and supports local businesses!

  Delivery is a flat $4.50 rate with all orders paid for in advance. Delivery is around 1pm to 9pm, with confirmation emails, and a text notification when the driver is 15 minutes away.
